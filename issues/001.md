- BlueprintAction(id, name, kind, mandatory=True, conditions=[], allowed_tools=[])

- DeviationResult(rule, tool, severity, reasoning, judge_used=False)

- HITLDecision(run_id, tool, decision, mode, reviewer='unknown')
